chapter 2

writing on the wall

The Towers of the Network run high, stretching into the night’s eternal fog. A permanent neon glow rims the vectors of the buildings, a testament to the unceasing pandemonium encased within.

On the 89th Floor, Jaz wanders out of The Void – squinting, as his eyes adjust to the absence of strobing lights, and the vacuum silence of the hallway. He follows the carmine walls and worn carpeted paths towards the Sky Bridge, connecting Towers Two and Three – two thirds of the most notorious establishments in the red-light district.

He pauses in front of the sliding doors, feeling uncharacteristically courteous, as he drafts a quick notice to pre-empt his arrival, sending it off with a twitch of his fingers.

The fingerprint pad gleams as Jaz presses his thumb to the reader, the authorisation screen flickering before him before he is granted admission. With a slight smirk, then sigh, he steps through the doors, hoping to delay the all-too-swift trip to The Golden Chalice.
